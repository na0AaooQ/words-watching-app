Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how personal information and input data are handled in Kotoba Mimamori | Pre-post wording risk check for social media.

This service is primarily provided for users in Japan, but it may also be accessed and used from outside Japan.

1. Input text data

Text entered into the Service is not stored in the system. The Service performs AI-assisted text analysis, but entered text is not used for AI training.

🔒 Input text is not stored in the system or on the server. It is not used for AI or LLM training.

2. Access analytics

The Service uses Google Analytics for service improvement. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect access information, but it does not collect information that directly identifies individuals.

3. Changes to this Policy

This Policy may be changed without prior notice.

Please see the About page for developer and operator information.
