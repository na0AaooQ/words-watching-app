This service is for checking text that you intend to publish on social media or the internet. Please do not enter text you would not post publicly, such as confidential company or organization information, personal information, or other sensitive information.

The AI (LLM) analyzes the entered text and displays reference information such as potentially concerning points, possible aggressiveness, defamatory tone, emotional intensity, and suggestions for improvement. The result is only reference information, and the final decision about whether to post remains yours.
No. The tool does not prevent posting. The final decision about whether to post, based on the check result or your own judgment, remains yours.
